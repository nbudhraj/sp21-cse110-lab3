Day 1: "Monday Motivation"

April 5, 2021

Like most people, I often dread Mondays, but this Monday was a pleasant surprise, as I ended up having a somewhat productive day and actually enjoyed going to my lectures. On Mondays, I typically watch my MMW lectures and I also attended my weekly MUS 17 lectures/discussions. Both classes offered interesting insights on modern art forms, helping me contextualize the societal implications of art pieces. Today was also a special Monday because the NCAA Men's Basketball Championship was that evening. Although the game was not as entertaining as some of he past tournament games, it was still pretty fun to watch Baylor University dominate Gonzaga University, who were heavily favored to win the game. Overall, the inspiring stories about Hip-Hop and art I learned during my MUS 17 and MMW 15, along with the adversities the Baylor basketball team overcame to win the NCAA tournament, motivated me to be on top of my homework for the rest of the week.


Highlights of the Day

  • My MUS17 discussion, where my classmates and I discussed our personal relationship to Hip-Hop

    At the beginning our section, the TA played us an interesting and deeply personal song by rapper Jean Grae

  • Learning about Realism, Impressionism, Cubism, and Dadaism in modern art through my MMW15 lectures

    In class, we learned about the significance of Salvador Dali's famous painting, The Persistance of Memory


  • Watching Baylor upset Gonzaga in the NCAA National Championships


Lowlights of the Day

  1. Didn't sleep too well the night before, so was a bit fatigued throughout the day
  2. Finding out that I have a CSE 101 Quiz on Friday
  3. Falling behind on some homework

To Do List for Tomorrow:

To Do:
  • MMW Readings
  • Finish up CSE 101 HW
  • Watch movie for MUS 17

Day 2: "Temptation Tuesday"

April 6, 2021

Tuesday was a pretty fun day. Since the weather was nice in the Bay Area, I went on quick hike with my sister in between my classes. Usually, I'm a bit more productive on Tuesdays, but the nice weather was a bit of a distraction and as a result, I also fell behind on some work, which was a bit frustrating. Although I probably should have completed my school work with full focus, I decided to multi-task (Sorry Prof Powell). I watched the Golden State Warriors game while doing my homework, so once again, I gave into tempation of not doing my schoolwork. Along similar lines I gave in to the temptation of eating dessert even though I have been trying to cut down on my sugar consumption. As a result, I decided to call today Temptation Tuesday.


Higlights of the Day

Day 3: "Workday Wednesday"

April 7, 2021

Because I fell behind on work because of my unproductive Tuesday, I made sure that I was going to be productive on Wednesday. As a result, I got up pretty early and got to work right away. I finished and submitted my CSE 101 HW, while also making good headway on my CSE 110 lab. I took a quick break to shoot some hoops at the park with some friends. I then worked on a series of other assignments for some of my other classes and ultimately, was proud of the work I accomplished that day. I continued to remain disciplined and cooked myself some healthy meals that turned out to be pretty tasty. Overall, I used this day to get back on track from all the distractions, which was nice!
